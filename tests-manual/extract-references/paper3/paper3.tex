\begin{abstract}
This paper provides a comprehensive survey of renewable energy systems,
covering solar, wind, and hydroelectric power generation. We analyze
cost trends, efficiency improvements, and integration challenges for
grid-scale deployment. Our findings conclude that solar photovoltaic
systems offer the best cost-to-energy ratio for tropical regions.
\end{abstract}
