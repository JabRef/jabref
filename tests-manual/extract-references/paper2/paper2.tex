\begin{abstract}
This paper presents a novel deep learning framework for climate change
monitoring. Our approach is capable of predicting regional temperature
anomalies with 95\% accuracy. We analyzed satellite imagery data
spanning two decades to train models that identify deforestation
patterns and carbon emission sources.
\end{abstract}
